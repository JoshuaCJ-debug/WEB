News & Events

Stay up to date with the latest happenings in the Department of Computing & Informatics.

Latest News

March 10, 2026 Achievement

Students Win National Coding Challenge

A team of four students from the department emerged as champions in the 2026 Uganda National Coding Challenge held in Kampala. The team developed an AI-powered crop disease detection app that impressed the panel of judges from Microsoft and Google.

February 22, 2026 Announcement

Department Receives 5-Year NCHE Accreditation

The National Council for Higher Education (NCHE) has granted full accreditation to all five degree programmes offered by the department for the period 2026–2031. This affirms the quality of our curricula and teaching standards.

January 15, 2026 Achievement

Dr. Nakamya Publishes in ACM Journal

Head of Department Dr. Sarah Nakamya has published a landmark paper on machine learning applications for malaria prediction in the prestigious East African Journal of Science. The research was co-funded by the WHO Uganda office.

December 5, 2025 Event

Annual Hackathon Attracts Record Participation

Over 120 students from 8 universities participated in the 4th Annual Bugema Computing Hackathon. Projects ranged from fintech solutions to e-health platforms. The winning team received internship placements at Andela Uganda.
