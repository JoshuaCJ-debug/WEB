Admission Criteria

Undergraduate Entry Requirements

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes including Mathematics and English
  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with at least 2 principal passes (minimum of 8 points)
  • A pass in Mathematics at O-Level or A-Level is mandatory
  • Mature-age entry: applicants aged 25+ may sit the university entrance examination

Postgraduate Entry Requirements

  • A bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related field with a minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Two academic or professional references
  • A statement of purpose outlining research interests
  • Professional experience is an added advantage for MSc Information Systems

Application Deadlines

  • Semester 1: Applications close 15 July
  • Semester 2: Applications close 15 December
