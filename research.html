Research & Publications

Our faculty and students are actively engaged in cutting-edge research that addresses local and global challenges through computing and technology.

Research Focus Areas

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Developing intelligent systems for healthcare, agriculture, and education. Current projects include malaria prediction models and NLP tools for Bantu languages.

Cybersecurity & Network Security

Investigating vulnerabilities in IoT devices, mobile banking platforms, and campus network infrastructure. Collaboration with Uganda's National CERT for threat intelligence sharing.

Software Engineering & Systems Development

Research into agile methodologies, cloud-native architectures, and DevOps practices tailored for small and medium enterprises in the East African context.

Data Science & Analytics

Using data-driven approaches to solve problems in public health, urban planning, and education policy. Focus on open data initiatives and statistical modelling with Python and R.
